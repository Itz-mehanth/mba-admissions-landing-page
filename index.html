SSN MBA

Welcome to the 26th batch of MBA Program. The legacy and brand of SSN speaks for itself.

The MBA Program of SSN is for those aspiring students who are keen to build their managerial career. With specializations across all the functional areas of management, the program provides the depth of knowledge with contemporary relevance. The MBA program of SSN has wide acceptability by companies across different verticals of industry. The graduates from the school are most sought after by the companies due to their industry-ready orientation. If you wish to live your dream and stand tall, SSN is the place where you could nurture it. For more information about the program, please visit SSN School of Management, Chennai.

PROGRAM HIGHLIGHTS

SSN School of Management (SSN SoM) is part of Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering (SSNCE). The institution is NAAC A+ accredited and autonomous under Anna University. MBA Program is accredited by NBA for three years with effect from 2023. The institution is part of Shiv Nadar Foundation, founded by Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman, HCL Technologies. The MBA program is one of the flagship programs at SSN.

Industry oriented and hands-on exposure.

Our program includes workshops, internships, and real-world projects to provide practical experience and industry relevance.

Merit based admissions based on all India admission tests - CAT/XAT/CMAT/MAT.

Admissions are purely merit-based, ensuring a diverse and talented group of students from across the country.

State-of-the-art infrastructure with residential facility.

Our campus features modern facilities, research labs, and residential accommodations for an enriched learning environment.

Holistic development of student with academics and managerial personality attributes.

Our program emphasizes holistic growth, including leadership, communication, and teamwork skills alongside academics.

Specializations in five areas of management with contemporary outlook.

We offer five management specializations designed to meet current industry demands and prepare students for career success.

Academic Eligibility

  • X, XII, UG > 60%
  • No history of arrears in UG program. However, applicants with up to two history of arrears can apply for the program. The call for GD/PI in case of applicants with two history of arrears is at the discretion of the institution.

Admission Test Eligibility

  • CAT/XAT/CMAT > 60 percentile
  • MAT/ATMA > 80 percentile
  • TANCET/ Other State Level Admission Tests > 80 percentile
  • Applicants who have lower scores than above can apply to the program with existing test scores and can update the same application form with any other future dated admission tests. This application is valid till the admissions season is closed. This feature will help applicants to earn an opportunity for GD/PI.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What specializations are available in the MBA program at SSN?

MBA Program at SSN is a dual specialization program. The student has a choice from five specializations: Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Operations & Supply Chain, and Business Analytics.

What are the admission requirements for the MBA program?

A consistent academic record with good percentiles in CAT/XAT/CMAT/MAT will qualify you for a call for GD/PI.

Is there a work experience requirement for MBA applicants?

Work experience of over 24 months will add value to your learning, but it is not mandatory. Around 75-80% of the batch consists of fresh graduates.

Are there opportunities for internships during the MBA program?

The companies are keen to offer internships before considering students for roles. The institution provides opportunities that align with student expectations.

What is the tuition fee for the MBA program, and are there financial aid options?

The MBA program costs approximately Rs. 6.5 lacs (inclusive of single room hostel stay). Banks support graduates through merit-based admissions.

How does SSN support MBA students in career placement?

A full-fledged career development center provides career support 24/7. SSN’s MBA program includes structured career training aligned with job descriptions on campus.

What networking opportunities are available for MBA students?

SSN has strong industry connections, with over 100 companies engaging with graduates, and a network of 2000+ alumni to support career development.

Can I pursue the MBA program on a part-time basis?

The MBA program at SSN is a full-time, mandatory residential program.

What is the application deadline for the MBA program?

The admission window is generally December 2024 - April 2025. Applicants are encouraged to apply early and update test scores as results are released.

Is Business Analytics available as a specialization in the MBA program?

Yes, it’s available. However, Business Analytics is focused on decision-making within functional areas, distinct from Data Science roles.

How current is the MBA curriculum at your institution?

SSN’s MBA program incorporates industry-relevant courses with a strong digital focus, such as digital advertising and CRM.

Are professionals from industry involved in teaching MBA courses?

About one-third of the MBA courses are taught by industry managers, with 50% of specialization courses in the third semester led by industry professionals.

What methods can I use to assess the return on investment (ROI) of the MBA program?

The program fee is Rs. 6.5 lacs, with an average salary of Rs. 7.3 LPA, achieving ROI in under 12 months.

Is the MBA program residential?

Yes, it’s a mandatory residential program, providing an excellent environment for peer learning and engagement.

What accommodations are provided for MBA students in the hostel?

Each student has a single occupancy room with an option to upgrade to an AC room for an additional cost.

Which University awards the MBA degree in SSN?

SSN MBA is autonomous under Anna University, and the degree is awarded by Anna University.

What are the good attributes of MBA graduates that SSN produces?

SSN graduates are contemporary, well-rounded, and well-prepared for managerial roles.

Can I study as a day scholar if I am from Chennai?

All students are required to stay in the hostel.

What is the duration of the internship window in MBA?

The program includes an 8-week summer internship and a 12-week project internship.

When will I get a call for GD/PI?

Generally, within 10-15 days after applying for the program.

What can I do if I do not get a call for GD/PI?

If you don’t get a call, consider retaking admission tests and updating your application to improve your chances.

How is SSN linked to Shiv Nadar Foundation (SNF)?

SSN is a part of SNF, which has invested $1.5 billion in education through Dr. Shiv Nadar’s contributions.

How does informal learning happen in the MBA program?

Informal learning happens through clubs, events, and participation in MMA and NHRD activities.

Why does SSN give preference to all India admission tests like CAT/XAT/CMAT?

A high score in national tests indicates a strong managerial career aspiration, which is crucial for learning and career success.

Is Marketing a largely preferred specialization in MBA?

Yes, Marketing is highly popular due to numerous job opportunities, with 80% of graduates choosing this specialization.

Are typical MBA jobs target-oriented?

MBA graduates are target-oriented as they work in decision-making roles across various functions such as marketing, finance, HR, operations, and supply chain.
